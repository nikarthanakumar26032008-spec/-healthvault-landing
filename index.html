How to install HealthVault on your phone

Android (Chrome): Open the app → tap the 3-dot menu → "Add to Home screen" → Add

iPhone (Safari): Open the app in Safari → tap Share button → "Add to Home Screen" → Add

The app will appear on your home screen like a native app — no App Store needed!
